About Us

We are two people who love video games and want to share their history! We want to show you how they've evolved through the years, and to demonstrate how each generation contributed to history.

Meet the Devs

Hugo Torres Brandon Varner
I have been a video gamer ever since I was 3 years old. Pac-Man was the first game I ever played, my cousin passed me the controller and let me play. Ever since then, I have been on the lookout for the newest consoles and games. I've been developing since 2021, but playing video games since I was 4 years old. I have fond memories of playing Duke Nukem 3D on my Dad's lap. I hope to become a full-time game developer one day.
