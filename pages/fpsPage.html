FPS Video Games

One of the most popular genres for how adaptive the genre can be, from telling war stories to telling stories about humans shooting aliens in outer space.

The genre started with a computer game called Maze War, which started off as a simple maze game, the developers realized that was quite boring so they then decided to add other players finding each other and shooting each other. From there on, it was the start of a new genre.

FPS started off as a simple vector graphics, in which it has now evolved into life-like graphics with storytelling of either war, or outer space conflict.

From there on, FPS has evolved into games that can be enjoyed with friends, whether through online, or couch co-op.

The most popular is either Fortnite, Call of Duty, or Battlefield. FPS is now being enjoyed by people of all ages, with now including cosmetics to personalize your character. To this day, FPS is still a growing industry, with many trying to reach popularity of millions of people.
