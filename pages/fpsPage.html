FPS Games

Skip to Video

Because of their versatility, first-person shooters have become one of the most popular genres. These games cover topics from telling war stories to telling stories about humans shooting aliens in outer space.

The genre started with a computer game called Maze War, which started off as a simple maze game, the developers realized that was quite boring so they decided to add the ability to shoot other players. This decision was the start of a new genre.

FPS games started with simple vector graphics, but they've evolved to include life-like graphics with storytelling of either war, or outer space conflict. These games can be enjoyed with friends either online or through couch co-op.

The most popular is either Fortnite, Call of Duty, or Battlefield. FPS games are now being enjoyed by people of all ages. To this day, the genre continues to grow with millions of fans.
